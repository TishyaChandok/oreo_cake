OREO CAKE RECIPE BROUGHT TO YOU BY ADA AND TISHYA!!

INGREDIENTS:

    1 cup= 250 ML..... 1 tsp= 5 ML
  • 120 gram Oreo Biscuit (2 Packets)
  • ½ Cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • Cream
  • Flour

    • METHOD:

      Take a mixer
      Add biscuits with cream in it and mix it till it becomes a fine powder.
      Add milk slowly and mix it again
      Add baking powder and give it a quick stir
      Then take a baking tray and spread flour all over it
      Add the batter on the tray
      Put your baking tray inside a hot pan
      let it preheat on medium flame for 5 to 7 minutes
      Bake for 30 minutes on medium flame
      open the lid and let it cool for a while
      Once cooled, invert the tray and gently tap to remove the cake.
      AND NOW YOUR OREO CAKE IS COMPLETE!!
